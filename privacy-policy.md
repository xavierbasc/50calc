## 1. Overview

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects—and what it does not collect.

**The DM50 App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.**

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, access, or share any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
- Usage data, analytics, or telemetry
- Photos, contacts, or any other data from your device
- Financial or payment information

---

## 3. No Internet Connection Required

The DM50 App operates entirely on-device. It does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any remote server, third-party service, or backend system.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect any data, it presents no privacy risk to users of any age, including children under the age of 13.
